Step 4 · Your Weird

Your weird is what makes you distinctly, irreplaceably you. This is where we lean into what sets you apart — not smooth it out.

What do you do, know, or bring that nobody else does in quite the same way? Think about your background, method, perspective, or style.

What's in your zone? List what your brand stands for, does well, and offers.

What's out of scope? What would make you cringe? Your don'ts are as powerful as your dos.

One sentence. What is the thing you do better than almost anyone? Start with "I have an uncanny ability to…" or "My superpower is…"

What are you fighting against? What's the thing in your industry, the world, or your clients' lives that you are trying to fix or disrupt? This could be a belief, a behaviour, a broken system.

Comma-separated adjectives. These describe how your brand feels. Mix warm with sharp, soft with bold — the best brands have texture.

What do your best clients say — in their own words — about the experience of working with you? Real testimonials or paraphrased are both great.

Step 5 · Your People

The people whose lives you want to transform with your work. The more specific you are here, the better your whole strategy becomes.

3–6 sentences in your own words. Her age, life stage, personality, what she's struggling with, what she's secretly hoping for. Write like you're describing a real person you love working with.

Optional — fill in what's relevant. Age range, location, profession, relationship status, how they use social media.

What are they struggling with that brings them to you? Be specific. What keeps them up at night?

What do they dream of? What's the transformation they're hoping for? Go deeper than the surface desire.

3–5 quotes your clients have said (or would say) about what they're going through. Verbatim is gold. These words will anchor your entire voice.

What stops them from saying yes? Time, money, confidence, trust? How do you address these?

Step 7 · Your Brand Story

The story of how you got here, where you're going, and who you're doing it with. This feeds your Brand Story, your Origin Story, and your future vision.

What are you most proud of in your work? What have you created, built, overcome, or been recognised for? These become the proof points in your story.

Where are you going? What does your future brand look like in 3–5 years? Dream big — this is your north star.

Optional but wonderful — tell us how you got here. What happened that led you to this work? The messier the better. This becomes the raw material for your brand story.

Who are you being compared to? Who would be your dream collaborators? What books do your clients also read? What podcasts do they listen to? This helps us position you in the right neighbourhood.
